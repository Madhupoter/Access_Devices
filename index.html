ONCOLOGY & INTERVENTIONAL ONCOLOGY

Access Devices is focussing its activities to provide innovative medical devices
that help improve the quality of life of Cancer patients in India.
Access Devices is the sole distributor in India for high technology organisations across the world, who are committed to providing state-of-the art products at affordable prices for cancer patients.
Access Devices operates through a network of distributors located in all major cities of
India with the corporate office in Bangalore.
